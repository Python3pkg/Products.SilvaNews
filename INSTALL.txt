Installing Silva News

  This package requires Silva 0.8.7

  Unpack the SilvaNews.tar.gz package into the Products directory of your Zope installation
  and restart Zope. Go to the Silva root and click on the 'service_extensions' object. Scroll
  to the SilvaNews entry and click 'Install'. The product should now be installed correctly.
